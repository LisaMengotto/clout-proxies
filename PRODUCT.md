# Clout Proxies MVP

## Core idea

People are judged by proxies.

Not just raw achievement. The signals around them. Reputation. Scarcity.
Association. Proof. Presentation. Momentum.

Clout Proxies turns that fuzzy game into a structured self-assessment, then helps
the user decide what to improve next.

## Who this is for

- Founders
- Creators
- Freelancers and consultants
- Job seekers with ambition
- Operators building personal credibility

The first wedge is people who already care about perception and advancement, but
do not have a clean way to audit the signals they are broadcasting.

## The MVP loop

1. User lands on the homepage and understands the value in under 10 seconds.
2. User enters name and email.
3. User fills in as many categories as they want.
4. User self-rates each completed category.
5. App sends the submission to an analysis endpoint.
6. App returns encouraging advice with the top 1-3 categories to improve next.

That is enough to test the idea.

## Product principles

- Optional, not oppressive. Users can skip categories.
- Specific beats abstract. The app should ask for details and proof.
- Encouraging, not shaming. The tone should help people act.
- Prioritized output. Users should leave knowing what to do next.
- Repeatable. Over time, users should be able to rescore and track improvement.
